OUR SERVICES

Hotel Bookings

Find your hotel or your apartment in UAE, give us your range, advice about your preferred location and inform us about your needs. We will fulfill all your expectation in relationship with our hotel partners. Our aim to make your stay unforgettable!

Tours & Excursions

Discover UAE with us, our tours will take you to discover the modernity and authenticity of Dubai through its innovative and futuristic skyscrapers, the old quarter of Bastakiya, the romantic creek, the colors of the Desert, the shining Gold Souq.

Transfers & Chauffeured Services

Adonis Dubai offers a range of transportation services at the highest levels of excellence. We proposed a fleet composed of latest vehicle models. Technical maintenance of our fleet comes as a priority while safety and comfort of our clients are always on our mind.

Meet & Assist at Airport

Adonis Tourism L.L.C representatives meet and assist you at airport. We welcome you to the UAE from Dubai airport, Abu Dhabi Airport, Sharjah Airport. Adonis Dubai Team offers a hot line valid 24/24, every client can contact us for any emergency case.

Desert Safari Experience

An adventure like no other, the desert safari will take you off-road into the desert for a thrilling experience in the capable hands of experts . Riding in a luxury 4X4, you will experience the wild majesty of The Arabian desert.

Dhow Cruise & Yacht Experience

Sail down in Dubai Creek or in Dubai Marina between city lights; explore the open sea few meters far from Burj elArab, The Palm or The World Islands. See a different Abu Dhabi from the sea and discover the fjords in Ras El-Khaimah close to Khasab and Musandam.


Always maintains a high, quality of service that meets its standards to guarantee the maximum tourist satisfaction to enjoy their vacations or business during their stay in the United Arab Emirates.
